Join the Photon Team

Photon is always looking to grow our crew with motivated sailors who want to be part of a focused, competitive, and collaborative racing program. We're building a team that shows up prepared, committed, and excited to push performance on the water.

Experience & Commitment

We welcome sailors with varying levels of experience, but some prior sailing background is required. More important than experience alone is reliability, curiosity, and a willingness to learn. Our goal is to build consistency—both in who shows up and how we sail—so crew members are expected to make most races and practices, or to be clear and dependable about their availability.

Team Communication

Just as we prepare thoroughly for racing, we take a structured approach to crew organization and communication. Team members are part of a dedicated WhatsApp group for coordination and updates, and a shared Google signup system for races and practices. Clear communication and mutual respect are essential to how we operate.

Training & Development

On the water, crew are trained to specialize in specific positions so we can sail efficiently and build trust as a team. At the same time, we rotate roles during practices to develop well-rounded sailors who understand the whole boat and can step in when needed.

Safety Requirements

Safety and preparedness are non-negotiable. Crew are expected to have appropriate gear for the conditions—ranging from PFDs and proper clothing to full foul-weather and safety equipment depending on the race.

If you're looking to sail hard, improve your skills, and be part of a committed, high-performing team on one of the fastest boats in Chicago, we'd love to hear from you.
